Different Gardening Techniques

Composting

Recycling organic waste into nutrient-rich soil. It improves soil fertility and reduces waste.

Read More

Mulching

Covering soil with materials to retain moisture, regulate temperature, and suppress weeds.

Read More

Propagation

Growing new plants from cuttings, seeds, or divisions. Expand your garden cost-effectively.

Seed Saving

Collecting seeds from mature plants for replanting, preserving varieties, and reducing costs. Ideal for vegetables like tomatoes, peppers, and herbs..

Natural Pest Control

Natural methods like neem oil, garlic sprays, or beneficial insects to manage pests without chemicals. Its safe for plants, humans, and the environment.
